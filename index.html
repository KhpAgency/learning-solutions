OUR STORY

Learning Solutions was founded in 2021. In Libya, learning solutions are the official partner and main representative of the German International University of Applied Sciences which was founded in 1998 in Berlin. After years of hard work, determination, goals, dreams and hopes of an ambitious team that recognized the power of education in our society with an aspiration to help young people employ education in the right way to achieve success. We succeeded in educating many students online and on campus.
